Okay, so who am I?

My name is Megan Nicius, and I am a creative, fun, loving girl with a big heart for adventure, volunteering, language learning, and engineering.

Travel? Where have you been?

After graduating from New York University in May of 2023, I went on my first international trip to Japan. It was a trip that was in the drafting stage for a while, but I was finally able to turn it into a reality.

But that's not all! A few months later, I took a spontaneous trip to Norway and France, where I reunited with old friends and made some new ones. This was truly an adventure: I went on gorgeous hikes, shivered in an ice bath, toured the home of Edvard Munch, and got stranded in a foreign country.

Sounds cool! And you volunteer?

Yup! I've been engaging in volunteer work since I was little, getting involved in organizations like Kids Against Hunger and Interact. More recently, though, I have been working as a Volunteer Software Developer for a non-profit organization, and occasionally as a general maintence volunteer at a Local Design/Construction Department.
